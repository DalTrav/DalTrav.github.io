About Me

Hello!

I'm a UX designer with experience in graphic design and have worked across various mediums, being both a photoshop ninja (digital) and a master of cut and paste (print). I love The idea of combining visuals that draw the user in with crafted experiences that keeps them engaged and active, connecting us with the products we use. 

My process typically starts with research followed by either hand-drawn sketches or a quick digital composition to help “visually test” an idea before moving on to one or a few of my best friends: Sketch, Adobe Photoshop, and Illustrator. When not creating, you can rarely catch me inside of a blanket fort sipping hot tea and catching up on a new favorite novel.
